None of the electronics given to us for recycling is processed for resale, and everything that could potentially contain sensitive data (such as cell phones or computers) will be shredded. One hundred percent of the equipment dropped off will be recycled and kept out of landfills. We welcome every individual to join us in this important effort to promote environmental sustainability.
