Due to rapid advancements in technology, many electronic products are getting obsolete within a very short period of time, creating large amounts of unwanted electronic products, or ‘e-waste'.

Our habit of replacing electronics like cell phones, computers and televisions faster than we really need to, has resulted in millions of these toxic items being sent to landfills, adding fuel to the global e-waste crisis.

E-waste has already become a hazardous threat in the present era and to our future.

 

In association with Attero Recycling, India’s only integrated E-waste recycling company, we provide complete e-waste solutions from collection to disposal for both – the formal and the informal sector in the Goa region.
