Our operation ensure safe transportation through closed-bodied trucks, full data security to clients, health and safety precautions for all employees, pollution-free facility and complete processing of e-waste with zero landfill.


Our partner company, Attero has attained ISO14001:2004 and OSHAS 18001:2007, leading us to follow their cutting-edge standards. Attero is the only e-waste recycling company in India to possess a MOEF certification and also CPCB and UKPCB license approvals. .


Attero has even been mentioned in the UN General Assembly. The below mentioned text is available on page 17, point no. 78 of the report


"The Special Rapporteur welcomes the efforts made by the Government to encourage the establishment of e-waste recycling facilities through a Public Private Partnership model. He welcomes, in particular, the establishment in Roorkee of Attero Recycling, the first e-waste recycling facility certified by the Ministry of Environment and Forests to collect, dismantle, segregate, reuse and recycle the various components of obsolete EEE. "
